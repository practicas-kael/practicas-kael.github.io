👤 Personal Pronouns - Los Pronombres Personales

Los pronombres personales son palabras que usamos en lugar de los nombres de las personas, animales o cosas.

Los Pronombres Personales:

I
yo
You
tú / usted
He
él
She
ella
It
eso (animales/cosas)
We
nosotros/nosotras
They
ellos/ellas

Examples:

  • I am Emi. (Yo soy Emi)
  • You are my friend. (Tú eres mi amigo/a)
  • He is my brother. (Él es mi hermano)
  • She is my sister. (Ella es mi hermana)
  • It is a dog. (Es un perro)
  • We are students. (Nosotros somos estudiantes)
  • They are happy. (Ellos están felices)

💡 Recuerda:

  • He = para niños, hombres y animales machos
  • She = para niñas, mujeres y animales hembras
  • It = para cosas, objetos, animales cuando no sabemos si son macho o hembra

✨ Verb "to Be" - El Verbo Ser/Estar

El verbo "to be" es el más importante en inglés. Lo usamos para decir quiénes somos, cómo estamos, dónde estamos, etc.

Las Formas del Verbo "to Be":

am
soy / estoy
is
es / está
are
eres / estás / son / están

¿Cuándo usar cada forma?

  • I → am → I am happy (Yo estoy feliz)
  • You → are → You are smart (Tú eres inteligente)
  • He → is → He is tall (Él es alto)
  • She → is → She is kind (Ella es amable)
  • It → is → It is a cat (Es un gato)
  • We → are → We are friends (Somos amigos)
  • They → are → They are students (Ellos son estudiantes)

💡 Regla Fácil:

  • I → siempre usa am
  • He, She, It → siempre usan is
  • You, We, They → siempre usan are

Formas Cortas:

  • I am = I'm
  • You are = You're
  • He is = He's
  • She is = She's
  • It is = It's
  • We are = We're
  • They are = They're

🎨 Describing Clothing

Structure: Subject + am/is/are + wearing + article + adjective + clothing

Common phrases:

  • I am wearing... (Yo estoy usando...)
  • He is wearing... (Él está usando...)
  • She is wearing... (Ella está usando...)
  • What are you wearing? (¿Qué estás usando?)

Important rule: Adjective + Noun

  • ✅ Correct: a blue dress
  • ❌ Incorrect: a dress blue

Blue
Yellow

🌤️ Weather and Appropriate Clothing

We choose different clothes for different weather:

☀️ Sunny/Hot Day:

  • Wear: t-shirt, shorts, sunglasses, hat, flip flops, swimsuit
  • Example: "Today is a sunny day. I am wearing shorts and a t-shirt."

🌧️ Rainy Day:

  • Wear: raincoat, umbrella, boots
  • Example: "It's rainy. I am wearing a raincoat and boots."

❄️ Cold/Snowy Day:

  • Wear: coat, sweater, scarf, gloves, hat, boots
  • Example: "It's cold. I am wearing a coat, scarf, and gloves."

💨 Windy Day:

  • Wear: sweater, pants, jacket
  • Example: "It's windy. I am wearing a sweater."

🇨🇷 Weather in Costa Rica

Different cities in Costa Rica have different weather conditions:

Common questions:

  • "What's the weather like in [city]?"
  • "How is the weather in [city]?"

Examples:

  • San José: It's cloudy in San José
  • Alajuela: It's stormy in Alajuela
  • Cartago: It's cold in Cartago
  • Guanacaste: It's hot and sunny in Guanacaste
  • Limón: It's rainy in Limón
  • Puntarenas: It's sunny in Puntarenas

Example conversation:

Person 1: "What's the weather like in San José?"
Person 2: "It is cloudy in San José."
